UPLIFT COLLATOR - LAA Enhancement Justification Tool
=====================================================
A browser-based tool for UK family law solicitors to build and export
professional PDF documents justifying Legal Aid Agency (LAA) enhancement
claims (the 15% uplift). Runs entirely in the browser — no server, no
data leaves the machine.

Open the latest version's index.html in Chrome. Currently v1.6 on GitHub,
v1.8 available locally. Password-protected for client use "West Pier".
